Fashion

Fashion has been something I've cared about since high school. For me it's less about following trends and more about figuring out what actually looks good and feels right.

I'm drawn to clean, simple fits — basics that work together without overthinking it. I don't spend a lot on clothes, but I'm picky about what I buy.

I mostly shop vintage and secondhand. You find way better stuff at a fraction of the price, and nothing is worse than wearing the same thing as someone else.

Fashion Shows

Beyond clothing itself, what really draws me in is the world of fashion shows. There's something about them that's hard to explain — the music, the lighting, the energy in the room, the way everything is designed to create a single feeling for about 15 minutes.

The ambiance is everything. A great fashion show isn't just about the clothes — it's about the soundtrack, the set design, the pacing of the models, the mood the whole thing creates. Versace and Schiaparelli do this better than anyone.

I've watched a lot of shows on YouTube and put together a playlist of my favorites below. These are the ones I keep coming back to — mostly Versace, some Fendi, Balmain, Mugler, and a few others. Put on headphones and watch one.
